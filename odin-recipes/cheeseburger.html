Cheeseburger

You can't beat the classic cheeseburger! This dish is a great summertime meal that's typically enjoyed outside surrounded by friends and family. Master this dish and you will have the entire neightborhood asking for more!

Ingredients

  • Hamburger bun
  • Ground beef
  • Sliced cheese
  • Toppings of your choice, popular choices include lettuce, tomato, onion, ketchup, and pickles.
    •

    Steps

    1. First, form your meat patty and warm up the grill.
    2. Next, cook your meat patty at a medium temp.
    3. Just before removing your burger from the grill, top it with cheese and allow it to melt. This is also a good time to toast your hamburger buns (optional)
    4. You can now assemble your cheeseburger, placing each item on your bun followed by the toppings of your liking.
    5. All done! You can now serve!
