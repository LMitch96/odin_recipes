Tomato Basil Pizza

Tomato basil pizza is a great option for a healthy, yet savory dish. This pizza is made up of a hearty base layer of crust, topped with some delightful ingredients that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Pizza crust
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese
  • Sliced grape tomatoes
  • Chopped basil leaves
    •

    Steps

    1. First, start by preheating your oven to 425 degrees.
    2. Next, begin applying an even layer of sauce to your crust.
    3. After that, sprinkle a light layer of cheese on top.
    4. You can now top the pizza with your prepped tomatoes and basil leaves.
    5. Toss your pizza in the oven and cook for 15 - 20 minutes.
    6. Let the pizza cool, then serve.
