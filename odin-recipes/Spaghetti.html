Spaghetti

Spaghetti is a filling and cost effecient meal. Not only that, but it taste great! It's easy to make and can be enjoyed by the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Meatballs
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garnish
    •

    Steps

    1. First, cook your noodles by boiling them.
    2. In the meantime, start cooking your meatballs in a pan or airfryer.
    3. Add your sauce to a pan to warm it.
    4. Combine all of your cooked ingredients.
    5. Top with parmesan cheese and garnish to your liking, then serve!
